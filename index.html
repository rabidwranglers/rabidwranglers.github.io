Don't take on those critters alone!

Rabid Wranglers Wildlife Removal, located in CNY, professionally and humanely deals with nuisance wildlife problems. Some of the critters we remove include, but are not limited to, bats, rats, raccoons, possum, skunks, squirrels, fox, coyotes, deer, mice, chipmunks, woodchucks, and pigeons. Our primary goal is to solve the problem without unnecessary stress on the property owner or the animals. So whether it is a full fledged bat exclusion or a single skunk that's digging holes in your lawn, give us a call and we'll deal with the critters and repair so you don't have to.
